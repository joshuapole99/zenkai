3. How We Process Your Data

3.1 CV and job description

We do not store your CV or job description. When you run an analysis, your text is sent directly to the Anthropic API for processing and immediately discarded after the response is returned. No CV or job description text is saved to any database.

The processing flow is:

  • Your text is sent from your browser to our serverless API (Vercel)
  • Our API forwards the text to the Anthropic Claude API for analysis
  • The AI response is returned to your browser
  • No text is stored at any point in this process

3.2 IP addresses and fingerprinting

IP addresses and User-Agent strings are combined into a hashed fingerprint (SHA-256) used temporarily in server memory to enforce rate limits. The raw IP address is not written to persistent storage. Fingerprints are cleared periodically. We do not track or profile users.

3.3 Email addresses

If you voluntarily provide your email address via the optional email capture form, it is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) until we implement a proper email list integration. We will only send you product-related communications. You can opt out at any time.

3.4 Reviews and feedback

If you submit a review, the star rating and text are sent to us via Formspree.io. We may display anonymised feedback on the website. We will not publish your name without your explicit consent.

4. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage (not cookies) to store:

  • sol_uses: Number of analyses you've run (for free tier tracking)
  • sol_lang: Your language preference (NL or EN)
  • ls_session_id: Your Lemon Squeezy subscription session (for tier validation — Plus or Pro)
  • sol_email: Your email address if provided (or “skipped”)
  • sol_reviewed: Whether you've submitted a review

This data stays in your browser only and is never sent to our servers unless explicitly described above. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU/EEA resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to restrict processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for email communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at joshuapole@live.nl. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens): autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • API keys and secrets are stored as environment variables and never exposed client-side
  • We do not store sensitive personal data (CV text, payment details)
  • Server-side rate limiting with hashed fingerprints protects against abuse
  • Subscription tier is validated server-side via Lemon Squeezy API — cannot be faked client-side

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data in transit.
